Exercise 1

Two arrows are drawn on the plane. The first goes from (0, 0) to (3, 1). The second goes from (2, 4) to (5, 5). Do they represent the same vector?

Hint

A vector is determined by how far it travels in x and y, regardless of where it starts. Compute Δx and Δy for each arrow.

Answer

Yes. Both arrows have Δx = 3 and Δy = 1. Different tails, same vector.
